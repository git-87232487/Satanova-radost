Many Spiritual Satanist’s are unaware of the correct dates of the 8 Satanic Sabbats here in South Africa, and are left with those of America/Europe. Obviously, it does not make sense to celebrate the Summer Solstice in the middle of Winter, or vice versa, so I wanted to put up this list of correct dates. These were given to me in 2007 by High Priestess Pandora Arcana.

 

The dates are as follows:

1 February- Lammas/Lughnasadh

 

21 March- Mabon fall Equinox

 

30 April- Samhain

 

21 June- Yule Winter solstice (although it is perfectly fine to celebrate Yule in place of x-mas in Decemer)

 

1 August- Imbolc/Candlemas

 

21 September- Spring Equinox/ Ostara

 

1 November- Beltane

 

21 December- Midsummer Summer Solstice

 

And Father Satan’s special day remains as the 23 of December throughout the world.

I hope this serves to help Spiritual Satanist’s in South Africa!
